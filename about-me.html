About me

Hey thanks for stopping by to check out my portfolio.

I’m Ramanan based in Chennai, India who specializes in product and wedding photography.
I have loved photography since I was a teenager which urged me to pursue visual communication from Vels University. Here I was lucky enough to meet a number of amazing teachers who opened my eyes to the world of art and design.
After graduating, I took up intern with the industry titan Mr G. Venkat Ram, this experience altered the path of my life in more ways than I could ever imagine.

Now I work as an independent photographer focusing mainly of product advertising and wedding photography.

Check out some of my works and I hope you would like them.
